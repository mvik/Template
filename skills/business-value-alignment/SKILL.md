# Business Value Alignment

## Purpose

Ensure proposed work stays tied to a meaningful user, product, or operational outcome.

## Trigger conditions

Run this skill when:

- technical work grows significantly
- architecture discussions dominate
- infrastructure/platform work is proposed
- priorities are unclear

## Checklist

- Who benefits from this work?
- What changes for them?
- Why is this important now?
- What measurable outcome improves?
- Is this solving a real problem or optimizing elegance?

## Anti-patterns

- technology-first thinking
- elegance without user value
- infrastructure without consumers
- disconnected optimization
- work with no measurable outcome

## Output

Emit:

- alignment quality: strong/moderate/weak
- missing business connection
- recommended re-alignment
